FLIPPED OUT


how to play

The rules are simple, you will be given a set of images and one will be not quite right...


- you have to find the image that is upside down before time runs out
- the quicker you find it, the higher your score!
- the difficulty will increase as you progress through the levels
- click wisely! You only have 3 lives...
- you lose one every time you misclick.
- once you get to zero lives, you also lose.

Good luck and happy searching!
